Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account to apply?

No. Salone Ducks does not require login. You can apply as a guest.

What is an Application ID?

A unique ID sent to your email after submission. Use it to view, update, or delete your application within 5 days.

Can I change my application after submitting?

Yes, for 5 days after submission. After that, it's locked for processing.

How do I pay?

Pay at any Orange Money or Afrimoney agent using the payment reference generated with your Application ID.

Where do I collect my document?

You'll receive an email with the nearest service center location and pickup date.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All information is encrypted and handled by authorized officials only.
